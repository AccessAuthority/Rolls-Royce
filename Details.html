Rolls-Royce Spectre

Price Rs.8.99 - 10.48 Cr*

The Rolls-Royce Spectre is the luxury automaker's first fully electric vehicle, marking a significant milestone in its storied history. Unveiled in 2022, the Spectre seamlessly blends the brand's legendary craftsmanship with cutting-edge electric performance. Its design features a sleek and aerodynamic silhouette, characterized by flowing lines and a distinctive front grille that pays homage to classic Rolls-Royce elements, including the iconic Spirit of Ecstasy hood ornament. This modern aesthetic is complemented by an opulent interior, showcasing handcrafted leather, exquisite wood veneers, and state-of-the-art technology. The cabin creates a serene atmosphere with ambient lighting and customizable options that enhance the luxurious experience. As part of Rolls-Royce's commitment to sustainability, the Spectre exemplifies efforts to create an environmentally friendly luxury vehicle without compromising on performance or comfort. Positioned as a highly exclusive model, it reflects the brand's dedication to bespoke craftsmanship and personal expression, allowing each vehicle to be tailored to the individual preferences of its owner.
